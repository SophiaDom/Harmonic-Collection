Tompkins Park


In the center of East Village, Tompkins Park caters to all of it's influenial neighbors of artists, radicals, musicians, fashion lovers, etc.. With playgrounds for children, dog parks for animals and peaceful scenery for artists and passer-byers, this park is always serving a purpose to it's community. A place to admire nature in moderation and slow down New Yorker time.
