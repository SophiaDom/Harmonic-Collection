CENTRAL PARK

Central Park was developed with the intention of giving New York City a green space, but also for grabbing international attention. In 1853, 59th-106th Streets from Fifth to Eighth Avenues was bought for the park. " The landscapes are manmade and all built by hand."

Central Park is a maufactured experience with nature.
