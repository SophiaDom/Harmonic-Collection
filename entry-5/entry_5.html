NYC

Space between buildings themselves, between buildings and nature, and between nature itself.

Every opportunity of open space is filled.







Nature quietly takes over the city.

Human waste and trace consumes the city.







The buildings nest upon themselves.

An almost infinite sea of infrastructure.







Historic streets lined with trees.

Avenues lined with trees and cars beneath them.
