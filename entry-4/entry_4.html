CONCRETE JUNGLE













city persepctive


Endless pattern, design and life. City life volunteers nature connectivity for extreme life highs. An exchange.



People organized into these staggered buildings. Birds and crickets inhabit the brush.



The trees creep over the lowest buildings like overgrown weeds and moss around the edges of an adbandoned garden's stone path.
